About Me

Ruqayat holds a Bachelor's degree in Agriculture from the University of Ilorin, Kwara State. Since 2019, she has worked as a customer relationship officer at Crusader pensions. Ruqayat currently lives in Agbara.

In 2019, Ruqayat began her tech career with Utiva where she took the data incubator course in Data Analytics and went on to Information and Data analytics foundation to study Data Science in their fellowship program. Ruqayat believes that her time at IDAF was crucial in developing her interest in Software development despite starting out in Data Science.

When not coding or working, Ruqayat enjoys cooking,sleeping, watching movies and hanging out with her best friends.
